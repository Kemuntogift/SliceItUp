Menu

Meat Deluxe

The meat toppings include cooked and crumbled Italian sausage or ground beef, bacon, pepperoni, and sometimes sliced ham.

Hawaiian Pizza

Classic Hawaiian Pizza combines pizza sauce, cheese, cooked ham, and pineapple. Starts with our homemade crust then bacon.

Chicken Tikka

Chicken breast, BBQ sauce, fresh onions, cheeses made with 100% real mozzarella, provolone, and cheddar on a cheesy cheddar crust.

Veggie Pizza

Veggie is fresh and full of flavor, featuring cherry tomatoes, artichoke, bell pepper, olives, red onion and some baby spinach.

Pepperoni Pizza

Pepperoni is an American variety of spicy salami made from cured pork and beef seasoned with paprika or other chili pepper.

Cheese Pizza

While the combinations of cheeses used for a four-cheese pizza vary, you'll usually find both mozzarella and parmesan.
